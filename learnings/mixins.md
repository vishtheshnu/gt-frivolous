# I HATE MIXINS

Okay, not really.

But even the smallest thing makes them break and logs are near
useless in figuring out the cause.

Turns out when updating between Gregtech versions 7.2.0 and 7.2.1, 
you have to update JEI from 105 to 115,
otherwise your addon can't read your mixin config!
